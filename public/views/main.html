About Tech Connect

TechConnect is the one-stop-shop for information regarding the Charleston tech community. It features job opportunities, highlights Charleston tech companies, and advertises opportunities for professionals to collaborate and connect.

New to the area? Just want to know more about Charleston, SC tech companies? TechConnect allows you to see all of the companies in a list or on a map. Click the button below to see all of the Charleston tech companies now.
